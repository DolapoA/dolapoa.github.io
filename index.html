Bioinformatics & Data Science in the area of Genomics.

Dolapo Ajayi, BSc MSc.
Trainee Clinical Bioinformatics Scientist @ The Royal Marsden Hospital

Hello there, I currently work as a Bioinformatics Scientist in clinical cancer genomics. I've been training to become a registered Clinical Bioinformatician in the United Kingdom via the Scientist Training Programme (STP). I take particular interest in using Data science methods to understand, diagnose and prognose cancer pathology. My current research is about the detection of Homologous Recombination Deficiency (HRD) in Ovarian Cancer patients using genomic sample data. In this work and beyond, I explore the utility of Non-classical statistical methods like Machine Learning and Bayesian Analysis through experimentation.
